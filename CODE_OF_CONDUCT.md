# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
leash community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
public or private harassment, publishing others' private information, and other
conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the maintainers at **conduct@goderash.example**. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the
privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
